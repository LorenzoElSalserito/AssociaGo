# AssociaGo User Manual

Welcome to the official manual of **AssociaGo**, the complete management platform for associations (APS, ASD, ODV, ETS).
This guide has been written to accompany the user step by step in using every single feature of the software.

## 1. The Main Dashboard

The **Dashboard** is the landing point after logging in. It is designed to offer an immediate overview of the association's health status.

### 1.1 Header
At the top of the page, you will find:
- **Association Name**: The name configured in the settings.
- **Logo**: The official logo of the association (if uploaded).
- **Current Date**: Today's full date.

### 1.2 Key Performance Indicators (KPI)
Below the header, three main cards show vital data:

1.  **Active Members**:
    *   Shows the total number of members who have an active membership (not expired) and are up to date with payments.
    *   A green indicator shows how many new members have joined in the current month.
    *   *Note*: This data is fundamental to understand the association growth trend.

2.  **Treasury Balance**:
    *   Displays the current balance calculated by summing all income and subtracting all expenses recorded in the system.
    *   Below the balance, the movements of the current month are shown:
        *   **Green**: Total income for the month.
        *   **Red**: Total expenses for the month.

3.  **Upcoming Events**:
    *   Indicates the number of events planned for the future.
    *   Shows the immediate detail of the **next event** in the calendar (Title and Date), allowing you not to miss important deadlines.

### 1.3 Recent Activity
The central section lists chronologically the latest operations carried out on the platform, such as:
- Registration of new payments.
- Registration of new members.
- Creation of new events.
This log helps administrators keep track of daily operations.

### 1.4 Quick Actions
On the right, a blue panel offers shortcuts for the most frequent operations, avoiding having to navigate through menus:
- **Register New Member**: Directly opens the registration form.
- **Create Event**: Opens the form for creating a new event.
- **Record Payment**: Takes you directly to the Finance section to enter a transaction.
