# 6. Assembly Management (Assemblies)

The **Assemblies** section allows you to manage official member meetings, from convocation to minutes.

## 6.1 Assembly List
Shows all past and future assemblies, with status (Draft, Called, Closed) and date.

## 6.2 Creating an Assembly
The form requires:
- **Title and Description**.
- **Date and Time**.
- **Location**.
- **Type**: Ordinary or Extraordinary.
- **Quorum**: Percentages required for validity in first and second call.
- **Roles**: President and Secretary of the assembly.

## 6.3 Assembly Detail
Once created, the assembly has a dedicated dashboard.

### 1. Participants
- **Attendance Register**: Allows marking who is present.
- **Proxies**: Management of proxies (who delegates whom).
- **Quorum Calculation**: The system automatically calculates if the quorum has been reached based on attendees and proxies.

### 2. Motions and Voting
- **Add Motion**: Title and description of the proposal.
- **Voting**: Recording of votes (For, Against, Abstain).
- **Outcome**: Approved/Rejected.

### 3. Documents
- Upload and management of attachments (e.g., convocation notice, budget).

## 6.4 Minutes
At the end of the assembly, you can automatically generate the **Minutes in PDF**, which includes:
- Assembly data.
- List of attendees and proxies.
- Text of motions and voting results.
